Famous Players and Player Positions

Famous Players

In the world ranking of table tennis currently, Sun Yingsha (CHN) is the world’s #1 for women’s singles. For men singles, Fan Zhendong (CHN) is ranked first. In men’s doubles, Fan Zhendong and Wang Chuqin (CHN) hold first and in women’s Jeon Jihee and Shin Yubin (KOR) hold first. You can find more rankings here: WTT Rankings

Player Positions

In a team, let's say a school team, there are starters which are players who play in competitions between schools and individuals of each school. There are other players who can both be substitutes or starters in-training. A team of starters contains 7 players: 3 singles players and 2 pairs of doubles players. There can also be mixed doubles players, meaning that one player from a women’s team and one from the men’s team form a pair for doubles.
